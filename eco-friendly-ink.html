Using Eco-Friendly Ink Looks Great and Protects Water Supplies

When you think of France, you might picture the Eiffel Tower or the Louvre Museum, but France has recently led the charge to protect drinking water.

Did you know that ink traditionally used in product packaging can make water unsafe to drink? France created new environmentally friendly standards known as AGEC (which in French stands for “Anti-Waste for a Circular Economy”).

TotalMount adopted AGEC-compatible ink for all its products to reduce pollution and verified that AGEC-compatible ink can be used to achieve a premium appearance.

Using AGEC-compatible ink can reduce water-supply pollution while providing premium-quality graphics worthy of being featured in Apple Stores around the world.
